  • Who are the shareholders?

    Kindly provide the name(s) of your new company's shareholder(s) and make sure the percentages of shares add up to 100%. Please note you will later have to provide a Know Your Customer form for each name in this list.

  • Who are the beneficial owners?

    Kindly provide the name(s) ofyour new company's beneficial owner(s). A beneficial owner is defined as a physical person who benefits to >25% of the company's shares. Beneficial owners have to be forwarded to the registry by law. Please note you will later have to provide a Know Your Customer form for each name in this list.
